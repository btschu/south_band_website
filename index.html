Excellence is just the beginning!

About Us

At Cheyenne South High School Band, we believe in fostering a supportive and inclusive community where every student can thrive. We are dedicated to cultivating not only exceptional musicians but also confident, disciplined, and passionate individuals. Our program is built on the values of teamwork, perseverance, and the joy of making music together.

Join us as we continue our tradition of excellence and explore the exciting world of music. Whether you’re a current member, a future musician, or a proud supporter, the Cheyenne South High School Band welcomes you to be a part of our musical journey.

Our Ensembles

We proudly feature a diverse array of ensembles, each fostering unique musical talents and interests:

Marching Band

Known for our dynamic performances and precise formations, the South High Marching Band has received superior ratings at the State Marching Festival for several years running. We consistently earn caption awards for outstanding music and marching, making us a staple of excellence in Wyoming's marching band scene.

Concert Band

Our Concert Band brings a rich repertoire to life with artistry and finesse. Their performances at the Wyoming Music Performance Assessments (MPAs) have garnered superior ratings year after year, showcasing our commitment to musical excellence.

Jazz Band

Our program includes two exceptional Jazz Bands that consistently receive superior ratings at numerous jazz festivals across the region. Their ability to swing, groove, and improvise captivates audiences and sets a high standard for jazz performance.

Pep Band

Energizing the crowd and boosting school spirit, our Pep Band is a powerhouse of enthusiasm and talent. Whether at a game or a rally, they bring an infectious energy that elevates every event.

Achievements and Recognition

Our bands are no strangers to accolades and high praise:

  • Superior Ratings: Across all our ensembles, we have a legacy of superior ratings at festivals and assessments, highlighting our students' dedication and the quality of our music program.
  • State Marching Festival: Our Marching Band's continued success at the State Marching Festival is a testament to our hard work and passion for marching music, consistently earning top marks and special awards for our performances.
  • Jazz Excellence: Both Jazz Bands have a history of exceptional performances, earning superior ratings and standing out at regional jazz festivals for their technical skill and expressive artistry.
  • Solo/Ensemble Success: Our students shine individually as well, with many receiving superior ratings and accolades at solo and ensemble festivals. Their achievements underscore the depth of talent and hard work that define our band program.

Honor Ensembles

We are incredibly proud of our students who go above and beyond, earning spots in prestigious honor ensembles. Each year, several of our musicians are selected to perform in:

All-City Concert Band

This ensemble brings together top concert band musicians from across Cheyenne, providing an opportunity for our students to perform complex and challenging pieces in a collaborative city-wide setting.

All-City Jazz Band

Our jazz musicians frequently earn spots in this prestigious city-wide group, showcasing their talent in a diverse repertoire that highlights their skills in jazz performance and improvisation.

District Honor Band

Selected students participate in this regional ensemble, allowing them to perform with peers from neighboring schools and under the guidance of distinguished conductors.

All-State Honor Ensembles

Recognizing the best high school musicians in the state, these ensembles allow our students to perform at the highest level and collaborate with their peers statewide.

All-State Jazz Ensembles

These ensembles highlight the best jazz musicians in the state, where our students get to perform intricate jazz pieces and develop their improvisational skills.

All-Northwest Honor Ensembles

This honor brings together talented musicians from across the Northwestern United States, providing a platform for our students to showcase their skills regionally.

All-National Honor Ensembles

Representing the pinnacle of high school musicianship, our students have the opportunity to perform with the nation’s finest young musicians in these prestigious groups.
